Policies

The people of Altis should benefit from the policies selected by the governor, not suffer. Too many past governors have taken advantage of the communities hard work by enforcing outrageous taxes on the people for personal gain. I strongly believe that the people of Altis should NOT be taxed. I also promote the legalization of recreational marijuana.

Who I Am

Hi, I'm Kate! I was born and raised on Altis in a little town called Kavala. I have traveled to many places around the world but Altis is my home. If you are a resident of Kavala you probably see me quite frequently. I enjoy spending my days with the lovely people in my community and I hope to meet more of you.
